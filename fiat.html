Getting Started - Fiat
The process for buying bitcoin is relatively straightforward once we get passed the first hurdle which is getting setup. For this guide we’ll be using Muun Wallet and CashApp. However you can replace either with your preferred wallet (BlueWallet) and exchange (Robinhood, Coinbase, or HodlHodl)

Installing Muun Wallet

  1. Install Muun using the Google Play Store, or App Store.
  2. Open Muun, and select “Create a New Wallet”.
  3. Create a new pin (at least 4 characters)
  4. Click the “back up now” button at the bottom of the screen to backup your wallet.
  5. To send and recieve bitcoin, you’ll need a wallet address. In Muun, we can get this by clicking the “recieve” button,
  6. Next, below the QR code, click “Copy”.
  7. Done! Our wallet is setup, we’ll move over to CashApp.

Buying and Sending Bitcoin using CashApp

  1. Open CashApp, and select the “House” button in the lower left corner.
  2. Next click on “Buy Bitcoin” and then click the “Buy” button.
  3. For this example, we’ll buy $10 worth of Bitcoin, but if you’d like to buy a larger denomination feel free.
  4. Once purchased, you will see your balance increase on the bitcoin menu of CashApp.
  5. To send to our Muun wallet, click on the paper airplane in the right corner next to the “Sell” button.
  6. In the menu that appears, click on “Send bitcoin”.
  7. Enter the desired amount of bitcoin to send.
  8. In the “To” box paste in your BTC wallet address from Muun, and select the “Bitcoin wallet address” button.
  9. In the next screen, leave the default transaction price, and select “Send.”
  10. Done! You’ll see the sent Bitcoin in your Muun wallet shortly
